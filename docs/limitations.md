# Limitations and caveats

## Species scope

The classifier is trained on **seven species only**.
Predictions for other taxa are unsupported and may be unreliable.

---

## Call-type dependence

Some species exhibit diverse call repertoires.
Rare or atypical call types may be misclassified or flagged as uncertain.

---

## Data imbalance

Certain species (e.g. Otolemur spp.) are represented by fewer recordings, which may affect precision and recall.

---

## Intended use

This model is intended as:

- a research aid
- a screening or exploratory tool
- a complement to expert assessment

It should **not** be used as a standalone taxonomic authority.
